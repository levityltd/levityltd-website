Brand Identity

Levity created the brand identity for Melly Leaves Dainty Jewelry, shaping a delicate, handmade identity for beaded pieces available in San Francisco boutiques.

Client
Melly Leaves Dainty Jewelry
Project
Brand identity

Details

Brand Identity

Created a delicate, handmade identity for a dainty jewelry line built around beaded pieces, soft floral artwork, and a boutique-ready presentation.

Relief Artwork

The branding features floral designs created in collaboration with a print relief artist, who carved the motifs that appear prominently throughout the identity.

Photography Direction

Directed product photography to show the jewelry as delicate, wearable, and handmade, with close-up styling that highlights beadwork, texture, color, and scale.

Materials

The product line uses high-quality glass, stone, and metal beads, fade-resistant sterling silver findings, and durable, thermally treated beading wire.

Packaging

Selected light purple holographic bags and hand-stamped tags featuring the relief print artwork, extending the handmade floral identity into the customer packaging experience.

Availability

Available in San Francisco boutiques.
